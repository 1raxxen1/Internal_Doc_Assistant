# Incident Response

Severity 1 incidents are customer-impacting outages or confirmed data exposure. Page the on-call incident commander immediately and create an incident channel.

Severity 2 incidents degrade a major feature but have a workaround. Notify the owning team and update the status page if customers are affected.

Postmortems are due within five business days. They must list timeline, root cause, customer impact, action items, owners, and due dates.
